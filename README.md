# superstores-_report
analysis of superstore sales data using powerbi

![](https://github.com/Lokoyiruth/superstores-_report/blob/main/sales%20report.jpg)

The business generated $2.3M in total sales across 1,894 products, with a profit of $286.4K and a profit margin of 12.47%.

![](https://github.com/Lokoyiruth/superstores-_report/blob/main/SALES%20REPORT.png)

The West region is the clear leader (31.58%). The West and East  regions account for 61% of all revenue. These two regions alone drive $1.4M of the $2.3M total
and should be priority markets for retention and upsell programs. The South region is underperforming. Targeted regional campaigns or better distribution could unlock growth.



![](https://github.com/Lokoyiruth/superstores-_report/blob/main/Screenshot%20(73).png)
California and New York are the most valuable states across both sales and profit. These 2 cities dominate both sales and profit charts. 
This concentration is a risk — a market disruption in either state could significantly impact overall performance. Geographic diversification should be a strategic goal.

On the product side, Phones and Chairs are the top-selling sub-categories by a significant margin — together they account for nearly 19% of all revenue Phones is leading sub-categories but technology products typically carry thin margins. Worth verifying if Phones drive profit as well as revenue — Copiers and Accessories often outperform on margin.


![](https://github.com/Lokoyiruth/superstores-_report/blob/main/Screenshot%20(72).png)
The Consumer segment outperforms Corporate and Home Office, which makes sense for a retail-oriented superstore model with Home Office customers at ~19% of sales is the smallest segment but may carry better margins (fewer bulk discounts). 
The discount sum of $1.56K relative to $2.3M in sales. This is suspiciously low. Across $2.3M in sales that's a 0.07% discount rate — either discounting is tightly controlled (good) or the field represents a count rather than dollar value. Worth confirming the measure definition.
